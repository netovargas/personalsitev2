Notes on Building Better Habits

Practical takeaways from behavior design research

February 20, 2026 · by James Clear

A few practical takeaways from a recent book.

Three takeaways that stood out:

  1. Make the desired action obvious.
  2. Keep the first step tiny.
  3. Track consistency instead of intensity.

I also liked the framing around identity. The idea is to anchor habits to who you want to become rather than only to outcomes. In practice, that means small repetitions with clear cues and immediate feedback loops.
