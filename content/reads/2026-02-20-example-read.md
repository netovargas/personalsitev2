Three takeaways that stood out:

1. Make the desired action obvious.
2. Keep the first step tiny.
3. Track consistency instead of intensity.

I also liked the framing around identity. The idea is to anchor habits to who you want
to become rather than only to outcomes. In practice, that means small repetitions with
clear cues and immediate feedback loops.
